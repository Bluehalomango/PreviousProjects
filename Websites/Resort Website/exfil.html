TIME IS OF THE ESSENCE

It was just meant to be a quick mission. In and out, a snatch and grab but things are never that easy. Somehow they knew we were going to be there. Maybe it was a leak, maybe it was luck, it dosen’t matter now. What matters is that you and your team are stuck in Pripyat, your bird has been shot down, the Russian’s will be there in an hour and you still don’t have the package. It’s going to be tight. Work with your team, locate your objective and find a way out before hell is let loose. You know the stakes. If they get to you, it’s game over. Don’t let that happen.

Difficulty: Hard Amount: 4 - 8 people
