A LOST CITY IN OUR BACKYARD

It wasn’t just the Chernobyl Nucler Power Plant that was cordorned off but the joint city of Pripyat. Since then, it has being a ghost town. Home to no one and no thing. Overrun by nature, it was left to rot and decay. But now, you can explore this anomaly of architecture. With our specially located Escape Rooms, you can experience all the misery of a town abandoned to time. Hear its cry, feel its pain. This is the Escape Zone.

A DEAD CITY, REPURPOSED FOR THE FUTURE

When we were defining our vision, we knew that we weren’t starting from a blank canvas. There still stood a city.So we made use of it. Turned old into new and breathed life into the dead. And now, we have this. A specifally designed section of Pripyat has been given a second chance. All new accommodation, built in the dead bones of the old world. Walk down the same corridors that families fled down, with no fear of whats to come. When you are here, you are under our protection. We built all of this, for you. This is the Escape Zone.
