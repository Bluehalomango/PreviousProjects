WINTER IS COMING

Winter is coming and you aren't prepared. You thought you would have more time. You thought you would make it back earlier. You thought the blizzard wouldn't hit until next week. But you were wrong and now, it may cost you everything. You and your team only have an hour until it hits and you know, if you're outside when it arrives, there will be no hope for you. The mission is still incomplete but you think you can finish it before the blizzard hits. You know you can't return empty handed, there won't be another chance like this for a long time. It's all or nothing now.

Difficulty: Medium Amount: 2 - 8 people
