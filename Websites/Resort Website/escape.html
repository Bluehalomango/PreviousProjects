THE DARK BELOW

How were you supposed to know they locked the bunker shut for a reason? You thought it was just old and abandoned but that wasn't true. This shouldn't be true but it is. Now they, whatever THEY are, are after you. Freak mutations, government experimentations, it doesn't matter. You unleashed them. And now they are after you. In only an hour, facility will be overrun and they will escape. Your life, and maybe everyone elses, comes down to this. You need to escape and you need to fix this.

Difficulty: Medium Amount: 3 - 7 people
