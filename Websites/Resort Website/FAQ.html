FAQ

Do I need to wear shoes?

If you want to have feet that you can walk on by the end of the experience, then yes.

Should I bring a Hazmat suit?

We supply you with our own in the escape rooms where they are neccessary. Do note, we can not promise the quality of said suits. Additionally, in the event of an apocalyptic event or situation class CLEAR SKY, we will repossess the equipment from you and prioritise it to our staff.

Do you keep our valuables secure?

While you go through the escape rooms, we keep all your belongings locked up nice and tight. Only you will have the key so only you will be able to get them. Just make sure to grab your stuff before you leave!

What happens if we go over the time?

Depending on how close you are to finishing, we may give you a grace period to finish it. Additionally, our response to your progress is also dependent on the Escape Room you are in. See our Terms and Conditions for further information regarding the responsibilities you have when you partake in our Escape Rooms.

What happens if we want to leave the Escape Room when we have already started?

As advised in our Terms and Conditions, we have a full commitment policy. That is to say, if you start an Escape Room, you have to see it through. Unless exceptional circumstances arise, such as a CLEAR SKY or NIGHTFALL class event, you will have to go through the entire experience. You paid for the experience and that is what you will get.

Do you provide returns or refunds?

This depends on the grace period you give us. Due to the time it takes to prepare each Escape Room and the level of depth and detail involved in them, we need at least a weeks notice to issue a refund. If you cancel plans with less than a weeks notice, we cannot guarantee a full refund.

Is there a dress code?

In truth, we believe it is wise to dress appropriately for your experience. In some cases, this is to enhance the experience but it is also for practical reasons, as outlined in our Terms and Conditions. For instance, it would be wise to bring medical equipment to treat radioactive poisoning to our 'Meltdown' room incase of failure or warm clothes to our 'Winter' scenario.
