I HAVE BECOME DEATH, DESTROYER OF WORLDS

You never thought it would actually happen but here you are. All out nuclear war has begun. It doesn't matter who shot first, the Americans, Russians, Chinese, French, it doesn't matter. What matters is that in a matter of an hour, hell itself will rain fire down across the globe. You weren't expecting this, none of you were. You were just a simple research team sent out for a check up on the Chernobyl Zone. You weren't trained and you certainly weren't prepared. But lucky for you, there is an old Soviet bunker located somewhere in Pripyat. The catch, you don't know where it is and you only have an hour but your team is depending on you. Time to start searching.

Difficulty: Very Hard Amount: 4 - 8 people
